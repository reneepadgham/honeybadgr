FAQ

How should I use Honeybadgr?

To grow your business you need talent that shares your vision. Honeybadgr delivers a curated pool of responsive candidates interested in virtual currency so you can spend less time on sourcing and more time interviewing & hiring.

How is Honeybadgr different from AngelList, Hired, and other recruiting tools?

Honeybadgr is 100% committed to improving the technical and non-technical talent working in the Bitcoin space. We only fill roles with early stage and VC funded startups who are Bitcoin centric. Due to this, we deeply understand your needs all the way from experience down to culture fit. Another thing that sets us apart is our background. We have years of experience sourcing and recruiting top talent from leading technology companies, both big and small. We are experts at engaging passive talent and converting them into your next hire. If you have a hard-to-fill opening, we will proactively source candidates for you to interview.

Who are your candidates?

We actively bring on Engineers, UX/UI Designers, Sales/Marketing and other critical startup roles with a passion for digital currency. We primarily focus on candidates in San Francisco Bay Area, New York City, Boston, Los Angeles, Seattle, Miami, Atlanta, London, Buenos Aires, Vancouver and candidates willing to relocate to these cities.

How does HoneyBadgr make money?

Honeybadgr currently offers two payment options: Traditionally, we charge 18% of the hire's first year base salary guaranteed for 90 days. Alternatively, you can choose to pay 2% of the hire's annual salary per month for their first 12 months of employment.

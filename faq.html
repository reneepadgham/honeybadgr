What if I don’t have a resume?

It has become very common to use LinkedIn and other social media outlets for maintaining work history and interests. Most times this will be completely adequate. However, there are advantages to a well though out and formatted resume. If you have one updated we'd love to review it and have available, should it be requested. It's not necessary to "polish up your resume" prior to joining Honeybadgr though. Share with us what you have and we'll go from there.
